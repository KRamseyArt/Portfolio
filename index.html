ABOUT

  • Software Engineer
  • Illustrator
  • Brand Manager

For as long as I can remember, my passion has been primarily driven by two contradictory forces:
Creativity and Logic
(I am a Gemini - duality is kind of my thing!)

As an artist for most of my life, I always took pride in my ability to bring my ideas to life. As I gained wisdom and experience, I discovered much greater fulfillment in helping others' visions become reality, working with teams towards a common goal.
Having worked within and managed teams throughout my career, I am always amazed by how much more a group can accomplish together, and love being a part of that constructive environment.
While I am constantly juggling a career and clients, my innate thirst for knowledge drives me to learn new things, ranging from bits of trivia to entirely new skillsets. I value my personal growth above all, and always make a point to travel, connect with loved ones, and maintain self-care. New experiences are my greatest motivators - the more challenging, the better!
